Help

This page is a tourist guide to Dunhuang. The home page contains photos of famous scenic spots in Dunhuang and two introductory videos. By clicking the link on the home page, you can enter three main parts: map, culture and tips. The left column shows pictures and text descriptions of Dunhuang's famous scenic spots, while the right column shows a tourist map. Culture is divided into four sections, which are city introduction, culture introduction, famous scenic spots and local cuisine. You can click to see the detailed introduction. Tips is divided into three sections, similar to culture, and you can click through to see the details.
